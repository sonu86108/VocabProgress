Guide to use this application....
1.This application lets you create custom vocabulary quiz.
2.You can create a quiz by selecting 10 words from main word section.
3.There are two ways though which you can enter words ,first is direct method where you directly enter words by clicking  plus button
or you can enter words by copying them to clipboard.
4.As soon as you copy a text via your device it will automatically get saved in this appilcation you can further provide the meaning or you
can provide meaning on the go by clicking on the notification's edit button which will be shown on text copy event.

Note:-
To use copy text feature , make sure that you have started the background service via main switch available on upper write corner.
